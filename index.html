BORDERLESS SOCIAL EXPERIENCE

You don’t have to be in an on-campus classroom to enjoy the social experiences that define university life. SP Jain’s Virtual Clubs are designed for students to create and enjoy social experiences around interests relevant to them, while networking and socialising beyond the classroom environment.

Social Clubs

Pick up some impressive bachata dance moves from a Latin American instructor. Learn a new language or have a Michelin Chef help you create your next culinary masterpiece…join students from around the world as you cultivate new interests, build new skills, and improve your network.

E-Sports Club

Participate in a new world of E-Sports, Gaming and Fitness activities along with a global community of like-minded students and fellow athletes – an experience never witnessed before

Business Clubs

Network with students from all across the globe and discuss relevant business issues through platforms such as Global Business Club, Fearless Debates, Women in Business, Business & Technology, Fintech and more.

Global Clubs

Leverage our global university partnerships to collaborate with students from around the world and engage in enriching cross-cultural interactions.
